This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit camoufly.me (the "Website"). Please read it carefully. If you have any questions, contact us at contact@camoufly.me.

Data Controller

This Website is operated by Kawaii Bounce F.R. (the "data controller" / titolare del trattamento).

  • VAT no. (P.IVA): IT01442860456
  • Registered address: Via Rismondo 65, Fivizzano (MS), Italy
  • Contact: contact@camoufly.me

For the purposes of applicable data protection law, including the EU General Data Protection Regulation ("GDPR"), we are the data controller of your personal information.

Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to personal information collected through this Website. It does not apply to information collected offline, or through our separate online store (shop.camoufly.me) or other channels, which are covered by their own notices.

Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the Website, we may collect:

  • Information you provide directly — if you contact us by email or form, we collect your name, email address, and the contents of your message. If you subscribe to our newsletter, we collect your email address. If you create an account, we collect the contact details you supply.
  • Information collected automatically — when you visit the Website, technical information such as your IP address, browser and device type, operating system, referring and exit pages, and date and time of access is recorded through server log files and through cookies and similar technologies.

Under the GDPR, some of this data — including IP addresses — qualifies as personal data even where it is not linked to your name. We do not deliberately collect special categories of personal data through the Website.

How We Use Your Information and Legal Bases

We process your personal information only where we have a valid legal basis under the GDPR:

  • To operate and maintain the Website and respond to your enquiries — on the basis of our legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f)), or to take steps at your request.
  • To send newsletters and marketing communications — on the basis of your consent (Art. 6(1)(a)), which you can withdraw at any time via the unsubscribe link.
  • To analyse Website usage and measure marketing — on the basis of your consent for analytics and advertising cookies (Art. 6(1)(a)), managed through our cookie banner.
  • To secure the Website and prevent fraud or abuse — on the basis of our legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f)).
  • To comply with legal obligations — on the basis of Art. 6(1)(c), where applicable.

We do not carry out automated decision-making that produces legal or similarly significant effects on you within the meaning of Art. 22 GDPR.

Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to operate the Website, remember your preferences, analyse usage, and support marketing. Cookies that are not strictly necessary — including analytics and advertising cookies — are only set with your prior consent, given through the cookie banner shown on your first visit. You can change or withdraw your choices at any time through the cookie preferences link in the banner. Strictly necessary cookies do not require consent.

Analytics and Advertising

Subject to your consent, we use the following third-party tools, which set their own cookies and may receive your IP address and interaction data:

  • Google Analytics 4 and Google Tag Manager (Google) — to understand how the Website is used.
  • Meta Pixel (Meta/Facebook) and TikTok Pixel (TikTok) — to measure and personalise advertising on those platforms.

These tools load only after you consent through the cookie banner, and you can withdraw consent at any time. Each provider processes data in accordance with its own privacy policy. We also use Google Fonts to display the Website's typography, which involves a connection to Google's servers.

How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information for money. We may share it with:

  • Service providers (processors) who help us run the Website and our communications, including our hosting provider, our email/newsletter provider, and the analytics providers listed above, which process data on our instructions.
  • Advertising platforms (Meta, TikTok, Google), which receive data through their pixels and act as independent or joint controllers under their own policies.
  • Authorities or other parties where necessary to comply with the law, respond to valid legal requests, or protect our rights and safety.

International Transfers

Some of our service providers (including Google, Meta, and TikTok) are based outside the European Economic Area, such as in the United States. Where we transfer your personal information outside the EEA, we rely on a recognised transfer mechanism — such as the European Commission's Standard Contractual Clauses — unless the destination country has been recognised as providing an adequate level of protection.

Data Retention

We keep your personal information only as long as necessary:

  • Enquiry/contact data — for a limited period after your query is resolved.
  • Newsletter data — until you unsubscribe or withdraw consent.
  • Analytics and cookie data — for the durations set by the relevant tools and described in our cookie banner.
  • Log files — for a limited period for security and diagnostic purposes.

When we no longer need your personal information, we delete or anonymise it.

Your Rights

If the GDPR applies to you, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you, free of charge.
  • Rectify inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Erase your personal information, in certain circumstances.
  • Restrict our processing, in certain circumstances.
  • Object to processing based on legitimate interests, and to direct marketing at any time.
  • Data portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format, in certain circumstances.
  • Withdraw consent at any time, where we rely on consent, without affecting prior processing.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at contact@camoufly.me. We will respond within one month, extendable by two further months for complex requests. We do not charge a fee unless a request is manifestly unfounded or excessive. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority; in Italy this is the Garante per la protezione dei dati personali (garanteprivacy.it).

Notice for California Residents

If you are a California resident, you may have the right to request access to or deletion of your personal information. We do not sell your personal information for money; however, our use of advertising cookies may be considered "sharing" for cross-context behavioural advertising under California law, which you can opt out of by rejecting advertising cookies in our banner. To make a request, contact us at contact@camoufly.me.

Children's Privacy

The Website is not directed to children, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 14 (the age of digital consent in Italy). If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us at contact@camoufly.me and we will delete it.

Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. Please avoid sending sensitive information through unsecured channels.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal or operational reasons. We will post the updated version on this page and revise the "Last updated" date above.

Contact

For any questions about this Privacy Policy or to exercise your rights, contact us at contact@camoufly.me.
